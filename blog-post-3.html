Sigurnost podataka i privatnost na internetu Data Security and Online Privacy

Mali vodič kroz osnovne navike koje štite naloge i podatke. A small guide to basic habits that protect accounts and data.

Najčešće prijetnje Common threats

Phishing poruke, slabe lozinke i “reuse” iste lozinke su među najčešćim uzrocima kompromitovanih naloga. Napadači često ciljaju ljudsku grešku, ne samo tehničke ranjivosti. Phishing, weak passwords, and password reuse are among the most common causes of compromised accounts. Attackers often target human mistakes, not only technical vulnerabilities.

Praktične mjere Practical measures

Koristi duge, jedinstvene lozinke i password manager, uključi 2FA gdje god možeš, i provjeravaj linkove prije nego klikneš. Redovno ažuriraj aplikacije i operativni sistem. Use long, unique passwords and a password manager, enable 2FA whenever possible, and verify links before clicking. Keep apps and your OS up to date.

Privatnost i digitalna etika Privacy and digital ethics

Privatnost nije “skrivanje”, nego kontrola nad sopstvenim podacima. Kao korisnici biramo šta dijelimo, a kao programeri imamo odgovornost da sisteme pravimo transparentno i sigurno. Privacy isn’t “hiding” — it’s control over your data. As users we choose what to share, and as developers we’re responsible for building transparent and secure systems.

Zaključak Conclusion

Male navike daju velike rezultate: 2FA, jedinstvene lozinke, oprez s linkovima i redovna ažuriranja. To je minimum koji pravi ogromnu razliku. Small habits make a big difference: 2FA, unique passwords, careful link checking, and regular updates. It’s the minimum that provides major protection.
